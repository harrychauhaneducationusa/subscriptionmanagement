# SubscriptionManagement

This repository contains the product strategy artifact for **SubSense AI**, an AI-powered subscription intelligence and recurring expense management platform.

## Contents

- `canvases/subsense-ai-brd.canvas.tsx` - the full enterprise-grade Business Requirements Document authored as a Cursor canvas

## What the BRD covers

- business problem and market opportunity
- product vision and target personas
- onboarding and UX strategy
- detailed functional and non-functional requirements
- AI and recurring-intelligence architecture
- bank aggregation and Setu AA considerations
- household analytics and savings optimization
- monetization, competitive positioning, KPIs, risks, and roadmap

## Viewing the BRD

The canonical artifact is the canvas file under `canvases/`. Open it in Cursor to review the structured, interactive document layout.
